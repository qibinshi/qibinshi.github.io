About

I am a geophysicist working at the intersection of earthquake physics, soil hydrodynamics, and data-driven seismology. My research develops and applies distributed acoustic sensing (DAS), machine learning, and large-N seismic arrays to study environmental processes, induced seismicity, and fault mechanics.

Research Interests

  • Agroseismology & soil hydrodynamics
  • Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS)
  • Environmental & near-surface seismology
  • Machine learning for seismic imaging & detection
  • Earthquake rupture physics
